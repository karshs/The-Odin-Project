World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is a classic, meaty, cheesy lasagna that has been perfected over years. It’s the ultimate comfort food for a Sunday dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 1 package Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 4 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • Homemade Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Brown the sausage and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving.
